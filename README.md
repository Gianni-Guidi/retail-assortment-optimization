# Retail Assortment Optimization & Portfolio Analysis

## Business Objective
Optimize product assortment by analyzing sales, volume, pricing, and distribution
to identify growth opportunities and rationalization actions.

## Dataset
Retail sales dataset including products, outlets, pricing bands, visibility, and estimated units sold.

## Analytical Approach
- Portfolio KPIs & Pareto analysis
- Value / Volume quadrant segmentation
- Assortment complexity & long tail rationalization
- Product visibility impact analysis
- SQL data modeling (SQLite)
- Interactive dashboards (Tableau Public)

## Key Insights
- Sales and volumes are highly concentrated across a subset of SKUs
- Significant productivity differences across outlets
- Clear candidates for assortment rationalization and re-pricing
- Limited impact of product visibility at portfolio level

## Technologies
- Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib)
- SQLite (fact & dimension modeling)
- Tableau Public (interactive dashboards)
